Dumplings

While I don't know how to create the filling, I know how to fold dumplings. You would have to cover the edges of the wrapping with egg or water, then add the filling. Afterwards, fold it like a burrtio and only connect the top bottom. For the sides, slowly fold it but don't stick it together. Push one side to the top and fold the other. I know it sounds complciated in text but it's easier to just to watch someone do it. Why don't you look at this tutorial . It's a little different from how I was taught but it's still relevant.
