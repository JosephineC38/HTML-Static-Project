Spots to Visit in NYC

There are many places to visit and explore in the city. There are many places that you shouldn't visit.


Great Spots

I tried several different bubble tea and the one I liked the most was L'Milkly. I went to its Flushing location and I was in love with its classic drink. I also went to its Chinatown location and it tasted roughly the same. I like that consistency because some other chains have completely different tastes depending on which store I go to. I haven't tried the other teas they have, I should one day.


Not So Great Spots

I don't hate the LIRR, I just hate waiting for the train. It used to fit perfectly with my schedule but now, I have to wait longer.
